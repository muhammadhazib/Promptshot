## 10. FAQ (SEO/AEO layer — written to be quotable by AI search)

**What is an AI product photography prompt builder?**
It's a tool that assembles a complete, detailed text prompt for AI image generators by letting you select your product type, platform, and visual style — instead of writing prompts manually or copying generic examples.

**Do these prompts work with Midjourney / GPT Image / ImagineArt?**
Yes. PromptShot outputs plain-text prompts tuned to work across major AI image tools, including GPT Image, ImagineArt, Freepik, Midjourney, and Flux.

**Is PromptShot really free?**
Yes — the builder is free with no sign-up. PromptShot earns through partnerships with some of the AI tools we link to (clearly marked), which keeps the builder free for you.

**What makes a good product photography prompt?**
Specificity: product material and finish, lighting setup, camera angle, background/scene, and — for ads — platform-safe composition so text and hooks stay visible. The builder handles all five for you.
